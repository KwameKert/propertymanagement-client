BELOW are the steps to pay by the MTN USSD Method.

  1. Dial *170#
  2. Select 2 (Momo Pay & Pay Bill)
  3. Select 2(Pay Bill)
  4. Select 5 (General Payment)
  5. Enter payment Code (The payment Code is UNI ‘space’ then type your PIN CODE) Note: With the payment code, type NSS in capitals then type your UNI PIN CODE. Don’t forget to bring one space btn the UNI & PIN CODE. Eg, [UNI KZRGAP8937419]. (Your Name shall be displayed as confirmation).
  6. Enter the amount which is GHC {{data.price}}
  7. Use NSS as your reference
  8. Enter your mobile money PIN.
